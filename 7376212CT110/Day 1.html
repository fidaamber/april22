BTS Park Jimin

South Korean Singer,Dancer And Song Writter

Park Ji-min (Korean: 박지민) was born on October 13, 1995, in Geumjeong District, Busan, South Korea.

Jimin debuted as a member of BTS on June 13, 2013,[8] and holds the position of vocalist and dancer in the group.

Jimin was awarded the fifth-class Hwagwan Order of Cultural Merit in 2018 by the President of South Korea, Moon Jae-in, alongside the other members of BTS for their contributions to the promotion of Korean culture.

Jimin's vocals have been described as delicate and sweet.

He is regarded as an exceptional dancer among the members of the group and in K-pop in general

He has cited singer Rain as one of his inspirations and reasons why he wanted to become both a singer and performer.[47]
